Welcome to Sustainable Gardening

Learn how to create an eco-friendly garden that supports the environment and thrives year-round. This guide provides practical tips for beginners to start gardening sustainably.

Eco-Friendly Gardening Tips

Composting at Home

Turn kitchen scraps into nutrient-rich compost to enrich your soil naturally. Use a compost bin to manage organic waste efficiently.

Water Conservation

Collect rainwater in barrels to water your plants, reducing reliance on municipal water sources.

Native Plants

Choose native plants that thrive in your local climate, requiring less water and maintenance.
